Welcome to the Mario Odyssey Website of Captures!

If you've ever played Super Mario Odyssey, then you'll know that you can take control of most enemies or objects for all-new moves. Here, you can learn about each individual capture, how to control them, where they can be found, and even fun facts! If you're up for a challenge, you can take a trivia quiz to see how well you know your captures!
